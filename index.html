To Tashmika

Tashmika, today I celebrate more than just your birthday — I celebrate you, and the ten years of memories we’ve shared. From the laughter, the adventures, the quiet moments, and even the storms — every single piece of our story has shaped the person I am today.

Even though life has taken us in different directions, my love for you hasn’t faded. If anything, time has only shown me how deeply you’ve touched my heart. You’ve been my best friend, my smile on hard days, my safe place, and my greatest ‘what if.’

I don’t know what tomorrow holds, but I want you to know this: you are still the most beautiful part of my world. More than a single day or plan, this is a quiet thank-you for ten years of moments — the inside jokes, the late-night drives, the soft pauses, and the way you’ve always felt like home. No matter where we walk next, those memories are the part of me that stays.
